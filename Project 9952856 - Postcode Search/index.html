Hello and welcome to HuntersWorld.co!

On HuntersWorld you can register as a dispatch hunter or a hunter that is registered at a basecamp. If you are located at the basecamp, please tick off if you are in the warehouse or on the road.

In warehouse, you can order a hunter to pick up something at the warehouse in your basecamp.

If you are a hunter on the road, you will get a message when a hunter in the warehouse want you to pick something up.
